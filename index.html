INTER-COLLEGIATE INNOVATION TOURNAMENT

The Chicago Innovation Chase is a national inter-collegiate innovation tournament
designed in collaboration with leading industry experts and academics in order to
prepare raw science, tech, engineering and mathematics talent to solve problems
that are relevant and urgent, so they can save the world.

We serve as the missing link in STEM education; Students are prepared to deal with
the increasing complexity of the industry by solving real-world problems within
spaces that mimic the multi-faceted, diverse, high pressure conditions in the world of
innovation. Change agents and experts from industry and academia help to develop
and test our curriculum each year, keeping it consistently cutting-edge, current,
and high impact.

DO YOU HAVE WHAT IT TAKES TO BE A CHASE COMPETITOR

What we are looking for is spunk, smarts, integrity, honor, quick thinking, restlessness,
creativity, good attitude, and mad skill. We are looking for IIT students who think
science is cool, who think design is important, who get bored easily, and who want
to save the world.

This isn't an engineering competition. This isn't a tournament to test your academic
ability. We aren't just looking for the Straight A kids. There is no minimum GPA to
participate in the Chicago Innovation Chase just like there is no minimum GPA to
save the world.
