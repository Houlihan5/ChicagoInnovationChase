Inter-collegiate Innovation Tournament

verb; to pursue with intent or to hunt or quest

noun; a colloquial nickname for the Chicago Innovation Chase; a national inter-collegiate innovation tournament designed in collaboration with leading industry experts and academics in order to prepare raw science, tech, engineering and mathematics talent to solve problems that are relevant and urgent, so they can save the world.

Why are we relevant?

We erve as the missing link in STEM education; Students are prepared to deal with the increasing complexity of the industry by solving real-world problems within spaces that mimic the multi-faceted, diverse, high pressure conditions in the world of innovation. Change agents and experts from industry and academia help to develop and test our curriculum each year, keeping it consistently cutting-edge, current, and high impact.
